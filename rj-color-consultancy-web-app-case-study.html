Color Consultancy Web App Development

the product

Colour Consultancy is a web app for customers and CC representatives of the aapkapainter, with tools to enhance their presentation of house colour visualization before the painting process starts in a more effective way.

PROJECT TYPE

Image Visualization tool

TIMELINE

6 months

My role

Lead Software Engineer

LOCATION

Bengaluru, IN.

MY ROLE

I created the design of the web app and as well as designed the APIs for it. Key Visual and the design of the platform itself, besides marketing pieces such as Landing Pages. I also provided the illustrations as an extra asset to speak to the audience better.

THE BRIEFING

As aapkapainter is a startup, the scenario is very much interesting because there’s a clear mindset alignment about better customer satisfaction via their service. They wanted visuals that behave as a code idea that can show the pre-demo of the customer's house.

THE CREATION I CAME UP WITH

By using the concept of the match between two parties (professionals and companies), the logo brings the letters D and P together, which were designed symmetrically. Bright green and dark purple create a perfect colour combination with the right amount of contrast. Modern typography, image mood defined by happy and engaged people, a refined set of icons and beautiful illustrations made the whole visuals very appealing.
